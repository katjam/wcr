Key areas of work include BPS (RPA) and CSS (Natural England) applications; Red Book valuations of a wide variety of properties for a number of purposes; fodder and straw agency by auction and private treaty; planning applications (new AOC houses, lifting AOC ties, new agricultural buildings, Class Q conversion of agricultural buildings, CLEUDs etc) and negotiation of option agreements and other strategic development agreements including land owner guidance before and during the process; landlord and tenant negotiations and property management (rent reviews, end of tenancy negotiations, creation of new tenancy agreements and licences, rent collection etc); compulsory purchase and other compensation claims including cycle paths; and inheritance tax and capital gains tax advice in conjunction with other professionals; land and farm agency including creation of Land Registry compliant plans

Lizzie Burton

West Country Rural Ltd, located at Coombe Farm Cottage, Cheriton Bishop, adjacent to the A30, has been recently formed by Lizzie Burton MRICS FAAV RICS Registered Valuer. The company provides a wide range of professional services to farmers and land owners across the West Country.

Lizzie has an established base of clients following her creation of a thriving professional services department at Exeter Livestock Centre and heading a similar department at a major regional firm in Exeter.

Lizzie has worked in a number of different regions in England and is able to draw on a wealth of knowledge, experience and professional contacts to find the right solution for your farming business or enhance the management and enjoyment of your property.
